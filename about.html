About Us
Amber Nuclear Solutions

The lead team of Amber Precast have formed a consultancy, Amber Nuclear Solutions, that supports Tier 1 and precast concrete customers with their extensive experience in the Nuclear Sector providing design solutions for complex decommissioning problems with cost effective, robust, flexible, sustainable precast concrete based products. Amber Nuclear Solutions work with precast concrete manufacturers supporting their nuclear aspirations providing differentiated products that comply with local and international statutory requirements and regulations.

Our products & services include the  "Con-tain Range" of Self-Shielded Concrete Containers, Shielding Blocks, Shielding Bricks, Fast deployment Interim Storage Facilities (ISF) and development and optimisation of customer specified bespoke or existing precast concrete designs and products.
